2) Partnership with Hong Kong Real Estate Agencies General Association

We are pleased to announce the launch of “Tenancy Screening” online services (www.dcm.com.hk/rea) jointly by HKREAGA and DCM on 1st November, 2022, developed exclusively for all members of HKREAGA.

HKREAGA was found in 1991 and has grown to become the largest real estate agencies association in Hong Kong. It now has about 2,000 corporate members and 16,000 individual members, which represent more than one-third of real estate practitioners in the industry.
