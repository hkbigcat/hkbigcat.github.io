集团简介

日新信用管理集团 – 45 年卓越功绩成就多元化的业务集团

DCM 专门从事风险和信用管理，成立于1977 年，正值香港迈进国际金融中心的时期。 80 年代以来中国经济的空前增长、全球化和信息革命带来了商业世界的范式转变。商机处处的同时，也满布风险，企业必需有良好的风险管理以应对新挑战。未能管理信用风险并采取适当的保障措施可能导致信用损失和业务失败。

DCM成功地把握时机，拓展业务，建立了全球信息和情报网络，和在中国全国范围内高效运作的信用管理业务。

凭借其经验和专业知识，DCM 专业化、加强、多样化和扩大其业务范围和服务，设立新的专业公司，以便在不断变化的商业世界中为客户提供多元和有竞争力的服务。

Our Mission

We are to serve our clients in the ever changing business world

Dedicated to becoming a world leader in credit risk management, commercial and market intelligence and China investments protection, DCM remains steadfastly committed to its first missions:

  • To provide quality assurance services in keeping with the principles of DCM: Dedication, Consistency and Superiority.
  • To maintain quality assurance services through continuous self-improvement, sound operational experience, advanced information technology, a prudent supervision system and a high calibre team.
  • To foster healthy business relationships among international investors and their Chinese counterparts through reconciliation of differences in culture, outlook, business philosophy and practices.
  • To help build a credit practice and culture in China so that both Chinese and foreign traders can invest and do business with more confidence and less risk.
