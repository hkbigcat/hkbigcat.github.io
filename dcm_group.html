DCM Group

45 years’ excellence advancing into a multitudinous business group

DCM was founded in 1977 specialising in risk and credit management when Hong Kong was in the formation of an international financial hub. Then, the unprecedented growth in China since the 80’s, globalisation and the information revolution have brought paradigm shifts in the business world. While creating business opportunities, so do risks. And needs for good risk management. Failure to manage such risks and adopt proper security measures may lead to credit losses and failure of the business.

DCM has successfully seized the moment and extended its services by building a global information and intelligence network and a highly efficient nationwide operation in the People's Republic of China (PRC).

Building on its experience and expertise, DCM specialises, strengthens, diversifies and widens its business scopes and services. New and specialist establishments have been formed to provide clients comprehensive and competitive services in the ever changing business world.
