DCM Business Consulting

Your trustworthy partner in fulfilling business ventures in China and worldwide

The company

When there are opportunities, there are risks. And needs for good risk management. In addition to credit risk management, a good business strategy is vital for any successful venture in China and worldwide. It allows you to concentrate your limited resources on the greatest opportunities to increase sales and achieve a sustainable competitive advantage. DCM provides consulting services in order to foster and match business partners with the setting up of DCM Business Consulting.

The services

  • Mergers & Acquisition - We advise clients on the conduction of SWOT Analysis, assess external factors, especially the impact of the economic climate and ensure access to the necessary finance and funding information. We will provide a target shortlist, approach, assess and evaluate the target business to help clients to make the right choice and avoid pitfalls during and after the deal.
  • Patent Registration & Intellectual Property Protection - We provide assistance to clients in the registration process, formulation and execution of long term country wide monitoring, litigation services, intellectual property valuation, and commercialization support. In case of counterfeit products, we assist in identifying violators, collecting evidence for possible legal action, coordinating with authorities in enforcing regulations and tracking down counterfeiters.
  • Commercialisation services - We match appropriate strategic partners, provide a platform for seeking partners and also formulate business strategic plans.

Other consulting services include IT Management, Logistics and Software Solutions, Eco-agri system, intellectual capital management and environment technology and management. ments.
