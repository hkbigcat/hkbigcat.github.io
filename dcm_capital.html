DCM Capital

The specialist in capitals and investments

The company

DCM has developed from strength to strength since its inception in 1977 and has extended its service worldwide, building a global information and intelligence network and a highly efficient nationwide operation in the People's Republic of China (PRC). It has been the bridge between the PRC and the rest of the world, providing its 5,000 prestigious clients the “Best-In-Class” advice and representation in: Private Equity, Fund Raising, Credit/Risk Management, Merger & Acquisitions, IP Rights and PR / Media Communications.

In 2016, DCM Capital was established as part of the international business group headquartered in Hong Kong with focus in capitals and investments. Striving to act as a legitimate conduit between China and the World, DCM Capital’s business covers Hong Kong, the US, the UK, and many other countries and regions worldwide.

The services

DCM Capital specialises in combining entity investment and integration of industrial and financial capitals, and comprises 5 sections:

  • asset and risk management,
  • wealth and investment management,
  • environmental engineering,
  • Hospitality and cultural tourism, and
  • public relations and marketing.

Gunaxi Management: key to business success in China

In China the right "Guanxi" makes all the difference in ensuring the success of a business. The right "Guanxi" minimises the risks, frustrations, and disappointments.

DCM Capital has built strong and efficient business relationships in China. Our “Guanxi” includes 174 state-owned enterprises from various business sectors including banking, infrastructure, construction, retail, hospitality, travel, cultural tourism and media. DCM Group has built one of the largest commercial databases in China which contains over 1.3 million company records, 55,000 enterprise credit reports and 560 industries statistics. Capitalising on the group’s resources, DCM Capital connects both the financial advisory and investment sides, and establishes a large central platform for the unified allocation of resources, providing secured, quality and trustable financial services for domestic as well as foreign clients by its experienced and reputable professional team.

One Belt One Road Initiative as the cornerstone

An insightful, safe and prudent financial advisor for domestic and foreign partners seeking opportunities with the PRC, DCM Capital achieves in particular a cornerstone by officially identifying, brokering, advising and managing win-win “One Belt, One Road” (OBOR) initiative for both foreign and domestic companies.

DCM Capital has been the international advisor to China Association For Promoting International Economic & Technical Co-operation (CAIETC) in the organisation of forums and in assessing and presenting investment projects for its members. CAIETCwas founded in 2014 by top executives of China’s leading business groups or Government bodies to accelerate the development & implementation of the economic co-operation with countries along the OBOR initiative areas.
