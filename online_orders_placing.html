Online Orders Placing

You can place orders or access to our Companies Records Index with your registered login ID & password. The Companies Records Index contains the name, address, incorporation/registration number of around 200,000 companies in Hong Kong.

Notes:

  1. When a writ record is filed in court, it will remain on the Court Writ records regardless of any subsequent discontinuance, outcome or way of settlement.
  2. The litigation record(s) shown in this report which is (are) for information purposes only may have been terminated, settled, paid or released before the compilation of this report.
  3. The current status and/or outcome of the above Court Writ can be obtained from the Plaintiff and/or Defendant in written requests.
  4. The ID number of a defendant in any civil litigation or bankruptcy is not open to the public, hence, defendants with names of similar spellings in different civil litigations may not necessarily be the same person. The same is applicable to unlimited companies which may bear similar names but different registration numbers. Hence, the check results are subject to client's verification of the relevant names.
  5. For any writs records verification, please contact us via email: dcmmkt@dcm.com.hk or customer hotline: (852) 2893 5128.
  6. BO = Bankruptcy Order (破產通知), AO = Annulment Order (撤銷破產令通知)
